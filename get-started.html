Choose a Program

National Housing Development

Welcome to National Housing Development & Urban Renewal Company Limited. Our company provides a range of homes under its housing options.
Some of the areas where these homes are constructed are located in Oliver’s, Five Island, Paynter’s, Denefields, and Dredge Bay.

Build On Own Land

Through this program, individuals who already own their
own land can have National Housing Development
construct a starter home design on their own property.

Land for Youth

National Housing Development & Urban Renewal Company Limited offers individuals to own a very piece of their own land from the age limit 18 to 35 years.
These lands will be available in Donavans. Land will be offered in different areas soon.
