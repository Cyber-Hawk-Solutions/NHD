Choose a Program

Build On Own Land

Through this program, individuals who already own their own land can have National Housing Development construct either one of our prototypical homes or a custom design home on their property.

Client Checklist:

  1. Job Letter
  2. Letter stating the size of house you would like to construct as well as any small modifications
  3. Proof of ownership (copy of deed and land register)
  4. Name of lending institution and loans officer's contact information
  5. Surveyor's diagram (Can be found at the Survey Department) Cadastral map (Can be found at the Survey Department)
  6. Copy of the Valuation / Engineer's report (Your bank will instruct you on when this report is to be created)
  7. Percolation test results

National Housing Development

Welcome to National Housing Development & Urban Renewal Company Limited. Our company provides a range of homes under its housing options.
Some of the areas where these homes are constructed are located in Paynter’s, Denefields, Friar Hill and Royal Gardens.

Process on how to register:

  1. Required to visit your bank or any other bank to seek pre-approval to make sure you’re the best candidate.
  2. After being pre-approved by the bank you will be given a letter that needs to be brought to National Housing Development.
  3. Once National Housing Development receives the pre-approval letter, you will be required to complete an application form.
  4. Once the form is completed your information will be carefully reviewed and entered into our database.
